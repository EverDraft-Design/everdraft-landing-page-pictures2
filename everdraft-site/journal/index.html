FIRST ENTRY

Why I’m Building EverDraft

Like many kids back in the early 2000’s (and now), I found myself waiting for the next Harry Potter book to release.

Having reread them SO many times, I was ready for something new but LOVED the world. This is when I discovered Harry Potter fan fiction and you could say I said good bye to the next few years of my life but I don’t see it that way. I had endless stories, set in the world I loved, characters I adored in different (and some times compromising situations that wouldn’t be cannon at all) all ready for my eyes to devour. And when I started feeling like the stories weren’t hitting right, I started writing my own.

Reviews and feedback kept me tapping my keyboard. Those readers will forever be in my heart with their words of encouragement, their laughter at my plots devices and the congratulations when the story came to its natural end.

I loved the community feel of the readers leaving such uplifting reviews of my ramblings; graphic designers who would happily whip up a story banner and send the image link as a show of gratitude, the beta readers who would happily give their time in exchange to seeing the fresh new chapter first.

Of course, I eventually grew out of fan fiction (not that we're going to say no here at EverDraft) but my love for reading and writing never dimmed.

Now to the personal problem at hand, it has been decades since anyone has read my writing and frankly, I miss it. I know I could post on similar sites and hope for the best. I could share it with my best friend but the idea of watching her face change as she reads my soul makes me squirm, regardless of how much I trust her. The internet is full of people with a range of differing interests, skills and passions. I want to find them; the readers who want to say, "I read this draft first!", the writers who find want to their tribe to build them up when they need it.

I want to fully immerse myself in this world again and with this in mind I have created everdraft.net. I envision EverDraft to be a positive and encouraging community of like minded souls all hoping to grow their creativity, one draft at a time. The readers, writers, betareaders, editors and graphic designers. So, help me get this idea off the ground! Sick of posting your stories and having it buried? Not likely to happen here (for now).


I invite you to join up to the waitlist and come along on this whirlwind adventure with me.

- Carley
